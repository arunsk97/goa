June monsoon note

June marks the beginning of monsoon in Goa. Here's what to expect:

  • Dudhsagar is at its most dramatic — but confirm jeep safaris are running
  • Water sports at Baga may be weather-dependent on heavy rain days
  • Far fewer crowds — restaurants and shacks feel intimate and relaxed
  • Carry a light rain jacket and waterproof bag for phones
  • Greenery is absolutely lush — Goa looks its most beautiful
